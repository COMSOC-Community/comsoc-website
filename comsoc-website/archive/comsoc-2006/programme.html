Programme

Jump directly to Wednesday, Thursday, Friday. The programme is also available as a PDF file.

The COMSOC-2006 programme consists of five invited talks and the presentation of 38 contributed papers, which have been selected from amongst the 48 submissions received by the programme committee. The slides of most talks are now available. We also have some photos of the workshop.

Registration: from Wednesday 08:15    Social Dinner: Thursday 20:00 (details)
Welcome: Wednesday 08:50-09:00 Farewell Drinks: Friday 17:15-18:30
