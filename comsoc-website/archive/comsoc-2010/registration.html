Registration

The registration fee is €30 for (PhD) students, and €75 for everybody else. This covers access to all talks (including the LogICCC Tutorial Day), lunches, coffee breaks, and a copy of the informal proceedings. We are also planning a social dinner which needs to be paid for separately (€30). If you are accompanied by someone who doesn't pay the registration fee but who wants to join us for the conference dinner, please mention this in the comments field during registration and pay an additional amount of €55 (which is the real price of the conference dinner) for that person.

Please register by 15 July 2010. Late registration is possible (at the same price), but for registrations received after this date we cannot promise to have enough copies of the workshop notes available, and we may also be short of places for the social dinner.

To register, please fill in the registration form. The registration fee has to be paid by bank transfer within one week after the registration. The bank account details will be provided during the registration process.

If you need an invitation letter to obtain a visa, please send an email to Jörg Rothe (rothe@cs.uni-duesseldorf.de).
