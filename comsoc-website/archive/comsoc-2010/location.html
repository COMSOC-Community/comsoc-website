Location

COMSOC-2010 will take place at the campus of the University of Düsseldorf (campus map).

  • Registration starts on Monday, 13. September 2010, at 08:30 in front of lecture room 3D in building 23.01.
  • All talks take place in lecture room 3D in building 23.01.
  • During the breaks, coffee will be served in building 23.01, in front of lecture room 3D at the lower floor.
  • Lunch will be served at the university restaurant Campus Vita in building 21.11.
