Travel

Düsseldorf has an international airport with direct connections to various cities. The university can be reached from the airport by taking the train (S-Bahn or regional train) to the main station (Hauptbahnhof) and then the tram number 707 or the subway (U-Bahn) number U79, direction "Universität Ost/Botanischer Garten, Düsseldorf".

Alternatively, the Köln-Bonn airport is served by many budget airlines and can easily be reached by train.
