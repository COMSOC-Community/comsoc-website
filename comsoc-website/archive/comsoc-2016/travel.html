Transportation

From Aéroport Toulouse Blagnac there are two possibilities to get to the city centre by public trasport:
  • Take tram line T2 direction "Palais de Justice" (the stop is right outside the arrival terminal), get off at "Arènes" and take metro line A to "Capitole" (30 min, one-way ticket for 1,60 EUR). From here it is a 15 min walk to the conference location.
  • Take bus "Navette Aéreoport" to "Compans Caffarelli" (20 min, return ticket for 15 EUR). From here it is a 10 min walk to the conference location. For the city center get off at "Jean Jaures".
There are public bikes available all around the city (credit card needed). A map of the public transport network can be found here.
