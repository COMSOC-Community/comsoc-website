Paper Submission

Thanks to everyone who submitted. We have received 24 papers. Submission is now closed.

Please follow the IJCAI-2011 formatting instructions when preparing your submission. Technical papers should be at most 6 pages long (in IJCAI format) and position papers should be at most 2 pages long (in IJCAI format). Submissions are not required to be anonymous.

To submit, please prepare a PDF file of your paper, a short abstract in plain text, and a list of two to five keywords. Then follow this link to submit your paper. You will be able to revise your submission any number of times before the deadline. It is also possible to first register a title and a short abstract and to upload the paper itself at a later time (before the deadline). Indeed, we very much prefer it if you register your intention to submit well before the deadline.
