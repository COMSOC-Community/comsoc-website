Related Events at IJCAI-2011

This workshop is part of the IJCAI-2011 workshop programme. Attendees may also be interest in some of the many (free) tutorials offered in parallel to the workshop programme. Of immediate relevance to our workshop are these two tutorials:

  • Cooperative Games in Multi-Agent Systems (Tutorial T2)
    Edith Elkind, Georgios Chalkiadakis, and Michael Wooldridge
  • Graphical Languages for Preference Representation and Applications (Tutorial T9)
    Sylvain Bouveret and Jérôme Lang
