Paper Submission

Thanks to everyone who submitted. We have received 48 papers. Submission is now closed.

Authors are invited to submit full papers not exceeding 14 pages in length (roughly 5000 words). Each paper should include a title, the names and contact details of all authors, and a short abstract of 100-300 words. When preparing your submission, please follow these formatting instructions. The easiest way of doing this is to use the Latex document preparation system with the class file comsoc.cls. We have prepared a sample file (sample.tex), which you can use as a starting point for your own paper.

Paper submission is electronic. To submit, please prepare a PDF file of your paper, a short abstract in plain text, and a list of two to five keywords. Then follow this link:

Enter the COMSOC-2006 Paper Submission and Reviewing System!

You will be able to revise your submission any number of times before the deadline. Please feel free to contact the organisers with any questions you may have.
